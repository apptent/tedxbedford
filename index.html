TEDxBedford App

Thanks to contributions for the participants at TEDxBedford on the June 15th 2013, the team from the BedfordTech Meetup Group built a mobile app from scratch on the day.

You can view it using a normal web browser, or install it to your mobile device following these instructions:

  • Click the link below to open the app in your browser
  • Before diving in to the contet, click to bookmark the page to your device's home screen
  • You should get a button on your device with tent as a logo
  • Click the button any time to enjoy the content (works best in landscape)

We haven't done exhaustive testing on all device types, so YMMV.

Thanks!
